TUSCAN RAGU

Guinefowl/Rabbit/Duck/Chicken 2Legs
Chop: Onion 1⁄2 -half clove of Garlic –
Half Carrot - Celery
Fresh Herbs (Rosmary- Tyme – Sage)
200 ml of Red Wine
Half spoon of Tomato paste

Start to roast the meet in a Pot on high heat ,
until is nice and brown outside. Add you
vegetable and tomato and cook for 2 minutes

and so add you wine.

Turn down on low heat and cook for about
90 minutes,or until the meat feld down from the bone.
